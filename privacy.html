Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Overview

Objective.ink is a Safari extension that improves the readability of websites by applying custom stylesheets. It is designed to work entirely on your device with no data collection of any kind.

Data Collection

Objective.ink does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, usage data, analytics, or browsing information. No data leaves your device.

How the Extension Works

The extension applies CSS stylesheets to supported websites to improve their visual presentation. All stylesheets are bundled with the app and stored locally on your device. A minimal content script runs on supported pages solely to signal the extension to apply its styles — it does not read, modify, or collect any page content. The extension does not communicate with any external servers.

Third Parties

Objective.ink does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

Permissions

The extension requires the following browser permissions to function:

activeTab — allows the extension to apply styles to the currently active tab when invoked.

tabs — allows the extension to detect when a supported page has loaded in order to apply styles and display its status badge.

scripting — allows the extension to inject and remove CSS stylesheets on supported pages.

storage — stores stylesheet preferences and enabled/disabled state locally on your device.

nativeMessaging — enables communication between the Safari extension and the native macOS app to read bundled stylesheets stored on your device.

webNavigation — allows the extension to detect when a page navigation has committed so styles can be injected before the page renders.

None of these permissions are used to collect, transmit, or share any data.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since no data is collected, there is no data to retain or delete. The extension stores only its own stylesheet preferences locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data.

Children's Privacy

Objective.ink does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.
